Research

I am currently working closely with Assistant Professor Prof Zhou Chao and Associate Professor Jussi Keppo.
Right now, I am researching utitilty maximization and optimization of information under uncertainty. Our research seeks to understand the impact of the decreasing cost of computing and data analytics. Key areas of research are optimization, finance and economics.

Teaching

I am also a teaching assistant for the Department of Mathematics at NUS, where I taught the modules MA1505 Mathematics I and MA1101R Linear Algebra I.
MA1505 provides a basic foundation for calculus and its related subjects required by engineering students. Topics include vector and multivariable calculus (think Divergence Theorem and triple integrals). Based on student feedback conducted by NUS at the end of every semester, I was one of the top three tutors in the department.
MA1101R is a first course in linear algebra. Major Topics include solutions of linear systems, subspaces, eigenvalues and eigenvectors. I taught a total of four classes, which translates to roughly 120 students. These include students not only in mathematics, but also from the Department of Computing and Department of Statistics.

THM Academy

THM Academy is an initiative I started to engage and educate students in Mathematics. My goal is to make learning mathematics fun and easy (or as painless as possible). My experience, both as a teacher and student led me to realise that all students, including myself, find learning mathematics difficult at some point in time of their education. I realise the reason for this is not having a proper mentor at that critical period of learning. It is more difficult to learn and derive new knowledge alone, than having someone teach it to you. It took many years of work and an apple tree for Issac Newton to develop his famous three laws of motion. Today, it is taught to 15 year old students in a couple of semesters.
Hence, I seek to be the best teacher I can be for my students. My method is based on Polya's problem solving techniques.
Right now, I have four students under my tutelage. Feel free to contact me if you wish to engage my services.

Auto Compile script for LaTeX

A Windows Powershell Script I modified to auto-compile any .tex files it finds in subfolders placed in the main folders. It checks if there exists a PDF file first, then if the TeX file is newer. If there is no PDF or the TeX file is newer, it runs latex.
